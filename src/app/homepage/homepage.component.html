Description

Vila Ana is an apartment placed in a small town called Njivice, located on the island of Krk, Croatia. It is placed within walking distance from the sea (aprox. 10 minutes). The apartment is suited both for family holidays, and for groups of friends who want to create new shared memories.

  • 4 Bedrooms (2 x double + 2 x 3/4)
  • 2 Toliets
  • For 4-9 people
  • Suitable for children
  • 2 Parking Spots
  • Pet friendly, * fee
  • Wi-Fi
  • Air Condition
  • Gas Grill
  • Private Garden
  • Hot Tub
